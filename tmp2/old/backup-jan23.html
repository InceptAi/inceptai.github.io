Deep Learning on the Blockchain

Inception Blockchain democratizes Deep Learning computations by rewarding Blockchain miners and users for contributing CPU/GPU resources to the Blockchain. Its a win-win for both the AI and the Blockchain community.

Learnmore

Blockchain Design

The Inception Blockchain brings together miners and users who contribute GPU/CPU resources to earn the InceptCoin (ICT). Using the cloud-agnostic tools that we provide, deep learning users are then able to train complex DNNs at scale on the blockchain.
