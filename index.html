Deep Learning on Blockchain

Incept AI democratizes Deep Learning by rewarding blockchain miners and users for contributing their CPU/GPU computing resources for AI model training and predictions. Its a win-win for both the AI and the Blockchain community.

Learn More

Inception Blockchain

The Inception Blockchain brings together miners and users who contribute GPU/CPU resources to earn the InceptCoin (ICT). Using the cloud-agnostic tools that we provide, deep learning users are then able to train complex neural networks at scale on the blockchain.
